As a learner of pixel art, I love using Procreate on my iPad with Apple Pencil.
But cleanly exporting crisp pixel art was a frustrating struggle - the only solution I found was tedious canvas and layer resizing in Procreate.

So I built [Pixel Companion][pc], now [on the App Store][pc_appstore].
It enables lossless upscaling, optimized specifically for pixel art to prevent blurring and artifacts.
The app also helps explore diverse color palettes online effortlessly.

I made [Pixel Companion][pc] to solve my own scaling issues as a pixel art learner. I hope it can assist other artists like you to to better create pixel arts.

I'm open for feedback to improve the app's usefulness. Please [let me know][email] how [Pixel Companion][pc] could better meet your needs!
