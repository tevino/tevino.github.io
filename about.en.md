## Me and this site

Hello, I'm Tevin, a lifelong learner with a diverse set of interests and a software developer with over a decade of experience.

Having worked with a range of companies from startups to Fortune 500s, I've had the opportunity to contribute my skills and knowledge in various business environments, learning and growing along the way.

After resigning from Microsoft, I decided to embark on the exciting journey of indie hacking, where I'm passionately pursuing my own projects and products. This transition has allowed me to explore my entrepreneurial side and continue pushing the boundaries of my creativity and technical expertise.

You can find my latest personal updates on [the "now" page]({% link now.en.md %}), check out my links on other platforms at the bottom.

Welcome to my blog, where I share everything I learned with my experiences and perspectives.

## Let's keep in touch

- I believe personal data is more important than any application[^data], and applications should respect that.
- I believe the longevity of plain text, it has survived time[^data].
- I believe the power of simplicity in technology and everything.

If these resonates, I invite you to join my contact list, for:

- **Insightful Updates**: Manual emails sharing blog news.
- **Exclusive Access**: Be the first to explore my latest [creations](/creation).
- **Two-Way Conversations**: I'll personally reply when you reach out[^email].

No automated marketing, no data sharing—just genuine connection[^spam].

Let's stay connected.

[^spam]: I don't use any 3rd party email platform, you receive **occasional** **manual** emails in **plain text** directly from me.
[^data]: There's [a blog post]({% post_url en/2023-07-25-data %}) about it.
